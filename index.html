onata browser
Time to be heard

An open platform created for every user, shaped by any user by contributing ideas, sharing designs and participate in development to ultimately have a browser forged by the true collective need.
We are a non-profit group of techies in a mission to produce a great browser in terms of user experience, human ergonomics and work efficiency with little to no sacrifice in performance.
Over the last 15 years we've identified the benefits and pitfalls of the ongoing browsers and we believe that we've managed to break down the formula of how to create a fully configurable browser that truly adapts to the user.
We will not accept funds as investment from 3rd parties, any admissible investment would be the time invested by the users in the Sonata community.
Visit our source code repository at github. Visit our forum for early requests and other information.
Our goal time line:
  • In 2 years, be in par with the best available browsers, both commercial and non-commercial.
  • In 5 years, gain significant advantage over any browser to date, customizability, performance and user experience would be unprecedented.
  • In 10 years, be the status quote for customization and standards compliance.
